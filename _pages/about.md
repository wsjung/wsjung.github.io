I am Woo Jung, a 1st year Ph.D student with the CSE Department at Washington University in St. Louis (WashU). 

Before starting my Ph.D program, I received a B.S. in Mathematics and Computer Science at University of Puget Sound (UPS) in 2020. I did a Summer research project with Professor Adam Smith on generating artificial mouse vocalizations at UPS. I was one of the last to study abroad with DIS in Copenhagen, Denmark in Fall 2019 before the pandemic hit. 

I am still exploring research areas but, currently, my research interests include machine learning applications, operating systems, and generative adversarial networks.
